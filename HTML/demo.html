What's halwa made of? It is made with either semolina or flour or lentils by roasting them in ghee and cooking with water or milk.
It is sweetened with sugar & flavoured with various spices like cardamoms, nutmeg, saffron etc. Lastly it
is garnished with nuts & a lot of times the halwa itself is cooked with nuts and raisins.
